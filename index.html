About BATU

Borg El Arab Technological University was established in 2022 as part of Egypt's technological universities initiative.

Located in New Borg El Arab, Alexandria Governorate, our modern campus spans 42,000 square meters with advanced facilities.

The campus includes educational buildings, labs & workshops, a library, admin offices, and student facilities.

Technological Education

BATU is part of Egypt's national push (Law No. 72 of 2019) to offer applied & technical education that aligns with labor market needs.
